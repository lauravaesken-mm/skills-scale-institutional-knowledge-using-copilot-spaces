# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
The QA Lead ensures that every release meets OctoAcme's quality standards by bridging the gap between technical requirements and user expectations. They own the quality assurance process from planning through acceptance.

### Responsibilities
- Quality assurance planning and test coverage strategy
- Alignment and validation of acceptance criteria with product requirements
- Coordination of manual and automated testing efforts
- Bug triaging and prioritization with the delivery team

### Goals
- Ensure all releases meet defined quality and acceptance standards
- Reduce defect escape rate to production
- Maintain clear and up-to-date test documentation

### Typical Communication
- Bug triage sessions and QA status updates
- Test plans and acceptance reports
- Collaboration in sprint planning and retrospectives

### Interactions
- **Developers:** Review test coverage, resolve blockers, and coordinate on fix verification.
- **Product Managers:** Verify that features satisfy defined success metrics and acceptance criteria.

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers own the path to production, ensuring that deployments are automated, safe, and observable. They are responsible for the stability and reliability of the infrastructure supporting OctoAcme products.

### Responsibilities
- CI/CD pipeline implementation and maintenance
- Deployment automation and infrastructure stability
- Release execution and post-deployment health monitoring
- Incident response coordination for deployment-related issues

### Goals
- Reduce deployment risk and time-to-production
- Maintain high availability and reliability of production systems
- Enable development teams through robust tooling and automation

### Typical Communication
- Deployment runbooks and pipeline documentation
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Infrastructure status updates

### Interactions
- **Developers:** Automate testing and linting within the PR workflow; support local development environments.
- **Project Managers:** Coordinate deployment timing, windows, and risk mitigation strategies.

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on the usability and accessibility of OctoAcme products, ensuring a customer-first approach is embedded throughout the design and delivery process.

### Responsibilities
- User research and interface design
- Creation of design specifications and user journey maps
- Ensuring accessibility standards are met across all features
- Conducting usability testing and integrating user feedback

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across product features
- Bridge user needs and technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design prototypes and specification handoffs
- Usability test reports and research summaries
- Design reviews and feedback sessions

### Interactions
- **Product Managers:** Shape product vision, validate usability requirements, and align on feature specifications.
- **Developers:** Provide design assets, implementation guidance, and review final UI implementations.

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
The Support Lead acts as the primary liaison between users and internal teams after a release, ensuring timely issue resolution, transparent communication, and continuous knowledge transfer.

### Responsibilities
- Post-release feedback collection and issue triage
- Incident escalation to the appropriate internal teams
- Documentation updates to reflect product changes
- Stakeholder and user notification during incidents

### Goals
- Minimize user impact from post-release issues
- Maintain up-to-date support documentation
- Ensure clear communication channels between users and internal teams

### Typical Communication
- Incident notifications and status updates
- Support documentation and knowledge base articles
- Escalation reports to Project and Product Managers

### Interactions
- **Project Managers:** Escalate critical incidents and coordinate stakeholder notifications.
- **Users:** Serve as primary point of contact for post-release support and feedback.

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts monitor success metrics and provide evidence-based insights that drive continuous improvement and informed decision-making across OctoAcme projects.

### Responsibilities
- Monitoring and reporting on project and product success metrics
- Providing data-driven insights to support prioritization and roadmap decisions
- Validating deliverable outcomes against defined targets
- Partnering with Product and Project Managers to measure project impact

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decisions across all project phases
- Surface actionable insights that improve product outcomes
- Maintain accurate and accessible metric dashboards

### Typical Communication
- Metric dashboards and performance reports
- Insights summaries shared with core project roles
- Ad-hoc analysis in support of planning and retrospectives

### Interactions
- **Product Managers:** Measure success metrics, validate deliverables, and inform roadmap decisions.
- **Project Managers:** Track project health indicators and support risk assessment with data.
